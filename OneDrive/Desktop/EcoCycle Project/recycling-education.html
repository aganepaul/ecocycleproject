Recycling Education

Why Recycle?

Recycling helps reduce waste, conserves natural resources, and saves energy.

What Can Be Recycled?

Common recyclable materials include paper, cardboard, glass, metal, and certain plastics.

Recycling Tips

  • Rinse containers before recycling.
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space.
  • Check local recycling guidelines.

Statistics

Recycling one ton of paper can save 17 trees and 7,000 gallons of water.
